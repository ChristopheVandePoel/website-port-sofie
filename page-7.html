Sofie Gagelmans (1995, Antwerp, Belgium) graduated during the fall of 2017 with a master’s degree in graphic design from LUCA School of Arts in Ghent. She received the jury prize for her master’s project and graduated with great honor. This webpage gives you an overview of her selected work and some various projects throughout her years as a student. Alongside to this, she tries to be on the lookout for new opportunities, individual assignments or collaborations with artists. Mainly operating in the field of printed matter, brand identities, posters, books and web design. Recently, some of her more current designs were selected and shown at different exhibitions last year. Her work was a part of the “Week of Design” in Kortrijk and of the “LUCA Showcase” in Ghent. Feel free to contact her for more information, questions or collaborations.


A serie of posters designed for a course that was held at Sint-Lucas in Ghent, with the support of Research Unit Image, Cinema Sphinx, CAMPO and 019. The lectures were a platform for social discussion, readings and documentary cinema. The posters are based upon the written documents that every speaker writes and memorizes before standing in front of an audience.
