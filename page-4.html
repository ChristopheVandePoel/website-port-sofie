Sofie Gagelmans (1995, Antwerp, Belgium) graduated in
the fall of 2017 with a master’s degree in graphic design
from LUCA School of Arts in Ghent. She received the
jury prize for her master’s project and graduated with
great honor. This webpage gives you an overview of her
selected work and some various projects throughout her
years as a student. Alongside to this, she tries to be on the
lookout for new opportunities, personal assignments and
collaborations with numerous artists. Mainly operating
in the field of printed matter, brand identities, posters,
book and web design. Recently, some of her most current
designs were selected and exhibited at two competitions.
Her work was a part of the “Week of Design” in Kortrijk
and of the “LUCA Showcase 2017” in Ghent. Feel free to
contact her for any question or collaboration.
